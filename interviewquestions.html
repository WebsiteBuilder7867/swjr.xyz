Interview Questions

Why do you want this job?

Describe the job you are applying for.

What qualifications and experience do you have?

Why do you want to work for this company as opposed to another company?

What do you know about our company?

What is the name of our CEO?

Describe a good manager.

Describe a bad manager.

Tell me about a time when you disagreed with your boss.

Do you work better on your own, or as part of a team?

How do you prioritise your work?

What would you do if someone in your team was not pulling his weight?

Do you have any hobbies?

How do you manage stress?

Why should we employ you instead of someone else?

What personal qualities do you have that would be beneficial to our company?

Where do you see yourself in five years time?

Would you be prepared to relocate to another part of the country, or another country, if we asked you?

Have you discussed this with your spouse?

Do you have any questions you would like to ask us?
