Reading Books

My best students like to read, it helps them to consolidate their grammar knowledge, increase and remember vocabulary and understand writing style. Always pay attention to the grammar structure and punctuation while you read, it will not only help you to understand text quicker and easier, but help you with your writing and speaking too!

The Sherlock Holmes series of books with audio: Sherlock Holmes Books

Bilingual books for Russian speaking learners of English: various levels: Билингва:слушаем*читаем*понимаем

Digital books with Russian translations for each sentence when you click next to it, various levels: Madbook

The Jungle Book - Level 6 and above with vocabulary: Jungle Book

Newspapers

The Guardian is a very popular free online newspaper among English learners at about level 7: The Guardian (UK)
