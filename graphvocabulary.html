Graph Vocabulary and Phrases for IELTS

Graph lines tend to rise, fall or remain steady but use the terms with the * whenever possible because they represent idiomatic speech and will therefore help raise your score.

The ones with * are very important, use if possible

picks up* - to increase from a base level
shoots up to* - to increase suddenly and at a high gradient
there is a sharp rise - to increase suddenly
rises steadily - to increase at the same rate
there is a gradual rise in - a slow increase
there is a significant rise in - a big increase
there is a slight rise in - a small increase rises to - reaches a level of
peaks at* - reaches its highest point
climbs to - reaches
there is a substantial increase in - a big increase
soars to* - climbs quickly to its highest point
surges to* - rises quickly to a high point
levels off at* - graph line becomes horizontal after a rise or fall stays constant at
stays at the same level
remains at the same level
fluctuates between a high of, and a low of - changing continuously
declines steadily to - declines at a constant rate
falls steadily to - declines at a constant rate
there is a gradual fall in - declines at a slow rate
there is a slight fall in - a small drop
decreases to - falls to
drops to - falls to
grows to
reduces to - falls to
a reduction in sales - amount of sales decline to
there is a downward trend** - a general pattern - always talk about trends when describing line graphs
went down
there is a dramatic fall in - a big decrease
dips to - goes down and up, bottom point
