Phrasal Verbs

A phrasal verb is a verb together with an adverb or a preposition. Phrasal verbs are a form of idiomatic speech and should be used in all parts of the test in order to fulfill the lexical resource marking criteria for level 7 and above. Remember to split the phrasal verb whenever possible in order to obtain a higher score. For example: instead of "put on the kettle" use "put the kettle on.
