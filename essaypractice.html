Practice Essays

The following practice essays should be between 250 and 265 words and take no more than 40 minutes. First write your essay on the official answer sheet which you can download here, then type it into Google Docs after you complete it, and send me the link for marking during the lesson. Remember to allow me permission to edit. If you don't know how to do this, click here for instructions. Note: you must have a Google account for this to work.

First read the question carefully so that you can give an answer to exactly what the question asks and nothing else. Remember to use a paragraph for the introduction, another for the main body and another for the conclusion. Use different tenses, modal verbs, phrasal verbs and conditionals as well as grammar structures such as "used to," "am used to," and "getting used to." You should also use some advanced vocabulary. Check your articles, tenses, punctuation and number of words when you finish.

The reason for using the answer paper is to find out how many lines of your handwriting constitutes 250 words. This will save you time counting words during the exam.

Practice Essay 1

The internet has been of great benefit to mankind, however, it has created new issues.
Outline some issues with the internet?
Explain how we can alleviate some problems that it has caused?

Practice Essay 2

Some people believe that traditional local markets and small shops are the best places to buy things, while others believe that big modern shopping centers are better.
Which do you think is the best and why?

Practice Essay 3

Some people believe that time spent playing videos on the computer is good for children.
Others believe this makes them less alert and able to function well in school.
What do you think? Explain your reasons.

Practice Essay 4

Nowadays we are producing more and more rubbish.
Why do you think this is happening?
What can governments do to help reduce the amount of rubbish produced?

Practice Essay 5

Some people believe that global warming is caused by human activity.
Do you agree with this statement?
What exactly might be the cause?
How can we solve the problem?

Practice Essay 6

Teenagers who commit serious crimes, such as burglary or murder, should be treated in the same way as adults who commit crimes.
Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of this and give your own opinion?

Practice Essay 7

In some countries, there is a problem with people becoming obese.
Do you think this is a serious problem?
Why or why not?
What can we do to either reduce the problem, or prevent it from becoming one?

Practice Essay 8

Space exploration is much too expensive and the money should be spent on more important things.
To what extent do you agree or disagree?

Practice Essay 9

Some people say that getting a good education is a ticket to a financially prosperous future.
Do you agree or disagree with this statement? Why or why not?

Practice Essay 10

There is a saying that history repeats itself.
Do you agree with this statement?
If so, why?
Is there anything we can do to prevent it?

Practice Essay 11

Some people say that the government should spend more money on renovating old buildings such as castles because they are historical monuments.
Do you agree with this statement, or do you think the money could be better spent elsewhere?
Explain your reasons
