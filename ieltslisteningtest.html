IELTS Listening Test

Four parts with ten questions in each.

Part 1 – Conversation between people – social context.
Part 2 – Monologue – social context
Part 3 – Conversation between two people in the context of education.
Part 4 – Monologue on an educational subject

The questions are in the same order as you hear the answers.

For example: The answer to question 1 will be heard before the answer to question 2 etc.

Accents from different English speaking countries.

30 Minutes listening and 10 minutes to transfer your answers to the answer sheet.

Many different types of questions: complete the sentence, multiple choice, label the diagram etc.
