General Writing Test

Two parts: both parts must be completed within 60 minutes.

Part 1- Letter, for example: explaining a situation or requesting information, 150 words, should take you 20 minutes.
Part 2- Essay: respond to argument, problem or point of view, 250 words, should take 40 minutes.
