How to Send a Letter or Essay using Google Docs

Copy and paste your text from Word onto your blank Google Docs page, then follow the instructions below.

Click on "Share."


Give your file a name.


Click on "Save."


Click on the little triangle and select "Anyone with the link can edit" from the dropdown menu. This will enable me to correct your grammar.


Click on "Copy Link" and paste it into Skype or Zoom chat. I can then click on the link and edit your letter or essay.
