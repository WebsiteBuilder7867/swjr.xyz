General Reading Test

Three parts: 60 minutes, 40 questions

Part 1 - Several texts: general factual information, advertisements, notices etc, based on social survival.

Part 2 - Two texts: based on the working environment.

Part 3 - One long text: prose from newspapers, magazines etc.

Many different types of questions.

Students generally find this test difficult to complete in time.
