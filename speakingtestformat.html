Speaking Test

Three parts:

Part 1 - Answer questions on topics such as home, hobbies, family etc., maximum 5 minutes.
Part 2 - Answer four or five questions from a card. Can sometimes be a follow-on question from the examiner. One minute to prepare + maximum 3 minutes speaking.
Part 3 - Discussion with examiner on topic of part 2. Maximum 5 minutes.
