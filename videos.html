Watch the videos and first try to understand without using the subtitles. If you do not understand, stop the video, go back over the part you did not understand and try again. If you still do not understand, view the subtitles or transcript. This is a very effective and interesting way to improve your listening skills. Remember to watch videos of people talking with many different accents, TED Talks is particularly good for this.
